Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data safe?

All processing happens locally in your browser. Your conversations are never sent to our servers or any third party.

Does it work with all DeepSeek conversations?

Yes, the extension works with all types of DeepSeek conversations, including code discussions, creative writing, and general Q&A.

Can I customize the PDF format?

Yes! You can choose to include or exclude timestamps, user names, system messages, and select different formatting styles.

Is there a limit to conversation length?

No limits! Export conversations of any length. Very long conversations are automatically paginated for optimal PDF viewing.
